About


How much should you charge for a night in your Airbnb? Every Airbnb host has struggled with this question. You don’t want to charge too little and be underselling your unique listing. You don’t want to charge too much and miss out on customers.

The Right Price Airbnb answers that question. This application allows you to input each aspect of your particular listing that makes it stand out. Right Price Airbnb then does the work of calculating the perfect price for your location and situation. Currently, this application offers support for hosts in six US cities.
